What Makes Our Pallets So Strong?

It starts with materials, smart design, and serious reinforcement.

High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE)

Made from a blend of recycled and virgin HDPE — known for its strength, chemical resistance, and ability to withstand extreme temperatures (-20°C to +60°C).

Internal Reinforcement

Pallets over 4 ton dynmaic weight are reinforced with steel rods to prevent cracking ad deflection under heavy loads or forklift impact — critical for long-term durability.
